Meet the Speakers & Mentors

CEOs. Olympic champions. Industry leaders. The voices shaping the next generation of executives.

CEO FIRESIDES

The CEO Fireside Series

Sitting CEOs join us across two days for unscripted, on-the-record conversations about strategy, capital, culture, and the moments that defined how they lead. No slides. No sanitized stories. Just the conversations rising executives rarely get a seat at.

2026 Lineup Includes

  • CEOs of Calgary-based and national enterprises spanning energy, capital markets, technology, and industrials
  • Leaders responsible for hundreds of millions to multi-billion in enterprise value
  • Operators who have led through expansion, downturn, M&A, and succession

2025 Featured Voices

Champions Summit 2025 featured Oliver Burkeman (NYT best-selling author), Dr. Radhika Dirks (AI expert), Brendan McCracken (Ovintiv), Jenna Kaye (Odyssey Trust), Manuj Nikhanj (Enverus), Iggy Domalgowski (Wajax), and Dr. Jeff Jolton, alongside board members, C-suite leaders, and founders in our Mentor Café.

Full 2026 lineup announced summer 2026. Applicants will be notified directly as speakers are confirmed.

Diary of a COO

A candid executive panel where leading COOs share the real-world practices, lessons, and trade-offs behind operational excellence. From scaling execution and improving systems to aligning teams and navigating complexity, this session offers an inside look at what actually works when leading high-performing organizations.

2026 panelists to be announced

Diary of a CFO

A candid executive panel where leading CFOs share the real-world practices, lessons, and trade-offs behind financial excellence. From strategy and capital allocation to risk, growth, and smarter decision-making, this session offers an inside look at how financial leaders help organizations perform, adapt, and thrive.
