CHAMPIONS SUMMIT 2025

Relive the Energy

This is not a conference. It is a standard.

Calgary invests in mid-level leadership and connects sitting CEOs to each other. The leaders 1–2 levels from the C-Suite — the ones replacing them in the next decade — are left to navigate the transition alone. Champions Summit was built to fill that void.

Inside the Room

Sitting CEOs. Senior operators. Future executives. The 2026 lineup brings together leaders who have built, scaled, and led through the moments that make or break an enterprise — and share what isn't in the earnings call.

How the 1.5 days are built

CEO Fireside Conversations

Sitting CEOs join us across two days for unscripted, on-the-record conversations about strategy, capital, culture, and the moments that defined how they lead. No slides. No sanitized stories.

Diary of a COO + CFO Panels

Two signature panels pull back the curtain on the roles that quietly run the enterprise. Operators and CFOs share the decisions, conflicts, and tradeoffs that don't make earnings calls.

Multi-Act Leadership Simulation

An immersive scenario running across both days that tests your executive decision-making under pressure. Teams form, navigate complexity, and present their calls to a room of peers.

Mentor Café

An exclusive small-group format where 20+ senior executives rotate through intimate conversations with rising leaders. Past mentors have included CEOs, CFOs, and board chairs across industries.

Who This Room Is For

Champions Summit is application-only. We curate the room of 150 leaders so every conversation matters.

Who This Is For

  • Rising executives 1–2 levels from the C-Suite
  • Operators carrying real P&L, team, or organizational responsibility
  • Leaders committed to building capacity, sharpening judgment, and lengthening their leadership runway
  • Cross-industry collaborators who value the room over the pitch
  • Returning Champions Summit alumni and peers nominated by them

Who This Is Not For

  • Service providers, consultants, or coaches treating the event as prospecting
  • Sales professionals using the room to build pipeline
  • Leaders more than two levels from the C-Suite (we'll point you to other Level 52 programs that fit)
  • Anyone whose primary intent is selling rather than building
THE CHAMPIONS CODE

This is not a conference. It is a standard.

Champions Summit exists to forge a community of current and future executives committed to responsibility over status, contribution over consumption, and long-term impact over short-term wins — for Calgary and beyond.

  • 01 We choose responsibility over status.
  • 02 We speak what matters, not what's safe.
  • 03 We contribute before we consume.
  • 04 We build beyond ourselves.
  • 05 We play the long game.

How we lead in this room is how we lead everywhere.
