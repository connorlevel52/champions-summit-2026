Apply to Attend

Champions Summit is application-only. We curate the room so every conversation matters.

This is not a conference for selling.

It is a room for building.

October 26–27, 2026 | Calgary, AB  ·  150 seats  ·  Investment: $1,499 per seat (invoiced upon acceptance)

Who This Is For

  • Rising executives 1–2 levels from the C-Suite
  • Operators carrying real P&L, team, or organizational responsibility
  • Leaders committed to building capacity, sharpening judgment, and lengthening their leadership runway
  • Cross-industry collaborators who value the room over the pitch
  • Returning Champions Summit alumni and peers nominated by them

Who This Is Not For

  • Service providers, consultants, or coaches treating the event as prospecting
  • Sales professionals using the room to build pipeline
  • Leaders more than two levels from the C-Suite (we'll point you to other Level 52 programs that fit)
  • Anyone whose primary intent is selling rather than building

If you are unsure whether the event is right for you, contact us before applying.

What's Included

  • Full 1.5-day summit access (October 26–27)
  • Multiple CEO Fireside Conversations
  • Diary of a COO + Diary of a CFO panels
  • Multi-act Leadership Simulation experience
  • Mentor Café: small-group sessions with senior executives
  • Curated dinner and networking reception
  • Champions Hour cocktails
  • Cross-industry peer network of 150 rising executives
  • Access to post-summit coaching and development opportunities

How the Application Works

  1. Submit the short application below.
  2. Our curation team reviews every submission. We'll respond within 7 business days.
  3. If accepted, you'll receive an invoice for $1,499. Your seat is secured upon payment.
  4. Capacity is capped at 150. Returning participants and nominated peers receive priority review.

Want to send a team? Inquire about group applications.

BEYOND THE SUMMIT

Your Seat Funds Classrooms.

A portion of every Champions Summit registration is donated to Classroom Champions, a Canadian charity co-founded by Champions Summit co-founder and Olympic Gold Medalist Steve Mesler. Classroom Champions connects Olympic and Paralympic athlete-mentors with K–8 classrooms to build goal-setting, perseverance, and resilience in the next generation of leaders.

When you invest in your own leadership at the Summit, you also invest in the next generation of leaders who will follow.
